Posh Bird

Illustrator, Photoshop

This was a logo design that I made for the company POSH BIRD LTD. The requirements set by the client were that he would like a font simliar to Times New Roman and that the logo had to have a bird in it, preferably a duck and to try and make the bird look upper class. I ended up using a colour pallete that was the same as the clients pet.
